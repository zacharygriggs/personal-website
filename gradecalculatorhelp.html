Grade Calculator Instructions (Back)
This tool can calculate what your grade will be in a class if you know what all your grades are,
as well as calculate what grade you need to receive on a specific assignment to get a desired
final grade in the class.

First, enter all known assignment scores and assignment weight into the fields. For assignment score,
you can either enter a percent grade (such as "80") or a score out of a total (such as "40/50").
For assignments you haven't taken yet, enter a question mark character ("?") to mark it as unknown.

Assignment weight is how much it counts for towards the total grade. For example if the final exam
is 30% of your grade, you should enter "30" as the weight for the final exam.

If you want to calculate what score you'll need on the unknown assignments to get a final grade in
the class, enter what grade you want in the "Desired Grade" field. This can also be either a percent
or a score out of a total. If you know all your grades, you don't have to enter a desired grade since
there are no unknown grades to calculate for.

Then press the Calculate and the tool will tell you what grade you need on the unknown assignments, or
what your known score is. You can enter multiple grades as unknown, but it will give you the unweighted
average you need on those assignments so it's recommended to only enter one unknown score for the best results.
