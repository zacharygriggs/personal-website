    • Senior design

    For school, I worked with two partners to create a senior design project. Our project was to create an Android Smartphone app to automatically take photos of potentially-concerning skin lesions and perform analysis on them to find certain metrics, like surface area, and whether it is symmetric or not.

    This app has a use-case for both doctors and patients; doctors could use it to quickly find hard-to-measure metrics like surface area in order to help with tracking and diagnosing, and patients could track their skin lesions at home without the need for a dermatologist appointment, which can have waiting times of several months.

    The app is not open source, but you can watch a video of how the app works, and see our poster that we presented at the senior design show. This poster was printed on a large poster board

    On the technical side of things, we hosted the images (encrypted) on AWS S3. We stored user accounts (password hashed) on AWS RDS in a MySQL database. The app was developed with Android Studio.

    I would several things differently with this project if I was creating it now with my currently knowledge. Ideally, the UI would be improved to require fewer clicks to take/analyze photos, the image segmentation would be improved by doing post-processing of the photos, and I would use services like AWS Cognito rather than writing my own login system.
